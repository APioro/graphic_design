For brands that actually mean it

At 21, I left my quiet village in the mountains of Poland and moved to the UK to study graphic design. It was my first step into independence, adventure, and discovering what kind of creative life I wanted to build.

Early on, I realized the design world often prioritizes speed, trends, and repetition over depth. That made me step back and ask the basics: who am I designing for, and why?
For me, it’s about authenticity and strategy. I treat every brand like it’s my own. I dive in, explore the world behind it, and stay flexible — whether that means learning a new tool, understanding an unfamiliar industry, or experimenting with a style I haven’t tried before.
