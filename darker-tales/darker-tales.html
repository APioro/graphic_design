Darker Tales

A magazine concept exploring the romantic and intellectual dimensions of the gothic alternative culture. I aimed to merge high-concept editorial design with atmospheric artwork, evoking themes of philosophy, mortality, and mystery while demonstrating refined layout, typography, and storytelling skills. The visual identity of the publication is built around hand-drawn ink pen illustrations, later scanned and digitally refined. Ink was chosen for its rich texture and its connection to gothic print traditions. The surreal, symbolic imagery is kept in a monochrome palette, paired with bold typography to create a consistent atmosphere. Spreads alternate between image-led and text-heavy layouts to maintain rhythm and balance.
