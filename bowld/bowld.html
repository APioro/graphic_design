Bowl'd

Bowl’d is a concept brand exploring how food design can feel personal, urban, and a little rebellious. Created as an independent study, the project reimagines the typical salad bar experience—trading cheerful greens and soft pastels for a darker, more experimental tone. It speaks to urban creatives and professionals who want food that feels personal, not performative. Bowl’d strips away the fake freshness of typical salad brands. It’s straightforward, a bit raw, and built around how people actually eat—different every day, a mix of what feels right and what does good.

The identity combines raw, tactile elements with modern minimalism. The hand-cut logo, intentionally imperfect, reflects the DIY spirit of customization. Ink-printed vegetables mix with real photography, creating a collage-like system that feels crafted and alive.



A monochrome palette sets the tone—urban, unpolished, and authentic—allowing the vibrant colors of the ingredients to stand out as the real heroes. Together, the visuals form a brand language that feels confident, grounded, and distinctly different from the polished, cheerful look typical of health food brands.
